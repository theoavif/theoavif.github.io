About

French artist based in Barcelona for over eight years. Working across analogue photography, collage and painting, Theo Avif explores memory, identity and our relationship to the real in an increasingly digital world.

Since 2022, he has developed a series of projects that blur the line between exhibition and experience — from painting to architecture photography, mixed media and collage.
